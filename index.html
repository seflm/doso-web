Features

Simple by design.

Ambient Flash Signal

A soft, rhythmic pulse that speaks to peripheral awareness — easy to notice, impossible to confuse with anything else.

Priority Beep Mode

For critical doses, enable acoustic alerts. A clear, calm beep that escalates gently — never jarring, always effective.

One-Press Confirmation

A single dedicated button stops the flash and logs the dose. Satisfying to press. Impossible to accidentally trigger.

Up to 6 Medication Slots

Attach up to six slots to a single DOSO — one slot per medication type. One device handles your entire regimen.

Configure via Website

Set up your schedule from any browser — no app to install, no account required. Plug in, configure, unplug. Done.

Extremely Low Power

Under 0.15W at full operation. DOSO runs continuously without meaningfully affecting your electricity bill — ever.

Environment Design

Your environment shapes your behavior more than your willpower ever will.

Research in behavioral science consistently shows that the cues surrounding you drive action far more reliably than motivation. DOSO is built on this principle: instead of asking you to try harder, it redesigns a small corner of your space so that taking your medication becomes the obvious, natural choice.
